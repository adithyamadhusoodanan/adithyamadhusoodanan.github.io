iBiG-SAM
Click here to download a zip folder containing all the m-files related to the computational aspect of the inertial Bilevel Gradient Sequential Averaging Method (iBiG-SAM) developed in our following paper. The algorithm solves convex simple bilevel optimization problems, i.e., scalar objective minimization problems over the solution set of another optimization problem.

Y. Shehu, P.T. Vuong, and A.B. Zemkoho, An inertial extrapolation method for convex simple bilevel optimization. Submitted and available on arxiv, 2018

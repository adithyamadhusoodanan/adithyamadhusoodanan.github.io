Job openings

  • Postdoctoral Position in Computational Optimization: I am currently recruiting a Postdoctoral Research Fellow to work on an EPSRC Project on developing a solver for bilevel optimization based on continuous optimization techniques which rely on strong mathematical foundations. The deadline for applications in 26 May 2017. You can drop me an email if you are interested or directly apply here
  • PhD Studentship in Nonconvex Optimization for Phase Retrieval: This is a multidisciplinary project between the schools of Mathematics and Physics & Astronomy that I am jointly supervising with Dr Marcus C Newton. It involves the development of efficient nonconvex optimization algorithms for the phase retrieval problem. To get some taste of the topic, you can have a look at this paper. The project is planned to start on 1 November 2017. Drop me an email if you are interested or directly apply here. The deadline for applications is 31 July 2017.
  • PhD Project on Hierarchical Optimization Under Uncertainty: The project will focus on two-stage stochastic bilevel optimization problems where upper level decision is taken before realization of uncertainty whereas lower level decisions are made after observation of uncertainty and upper level decision. More details can be found here. You can drop me an email if you are interested or directly apply here

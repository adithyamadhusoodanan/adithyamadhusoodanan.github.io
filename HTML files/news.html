Latest activities

  • The paper Solving ill-posed bilevel programs has now appeared in Set-Valued and Variational Analysis 24(3), 423-448, 2016. It provides a set-valued optimization viewpoint for bilevel programs with multiple lower-level solutions, which allows a generalization of all the currently known optimality conditions
  • I am currently organizing a stream on Bilevel programming and related topics for the 17th British-French-German Conference on Optimization that will take place at Imperial College London on 15-17th June, 2015. Email me if you are interested to give a talk in one of the sessions
  • The paper Necessary optimality conditions in pessimistic bilevel programming (with S. Dempe & B.S. Mordukhovich) listed in Optimization Top 10 Most Read Articles of 2014 by Taylor & Francis, 2015
  • The paper KKT reformulation and necessary conditions for optimality in nonsmooth bilevel optimization (with S. Dempe) has now appeared in SIAM Journal on Optimization 24(4), 1639-1669, 2014. It provides, for the first time, C, M and S-type stationarity conditions for bilevel programs in the nonsmooth framework
  • I was invited to speak at the 6th International Conference on Complementarity Problems in Berlin,  taking place from 4 to 8 August, 2014. Talks are plenary and by invitation only. Slides
  • The paper Necessary optimality conditions in pessimistic bilevel programming (with S. Dempe & B.S. Mordukhovich) listed in the Editor's Choices of Best Articles and made freely available, Optimization, 2015
  • The paper Sensitivity analysis for two-level value functions with applications to bilevel programming (with S. Dempe & B.S. Mordukhovich) selected as featured article by SIAM Journal on Optimization (online version) from November 2012 to June 2013

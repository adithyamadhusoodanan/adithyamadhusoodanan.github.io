A FEW WORDS


Dear all, having a diagnosis of a blood cancer is not the end of the road of life. This disease did not happen to you or your family due to any of your diet or habits in particular and science doesn’t have a single correct answer for the cause of it. However with rapidly advancing medical science, we have so many new targeted medicines for cancer which do not involve injections and side effects like hair fall and the new therapies have helped to conquer many blood cancers. So there is hope and future is bright. With lots of love and prayers...
                                    — Dr Anusree Prabhakaran

Journey of Dr Anusree Prabhakaran

Dr Anusree Prabhakaran is a native of Kerala, currently settled in Pune, Maharashtra. She completed her MBBS from Amrita Institute of Medical Education and Research Centre, Cochin as best outgoing student. She did her MD Internal Medicine and DM Clinical Hematology from the prestigious PGIMER Chandigarh. After completing her DM, she did fellowship in Tata Medical Centre Kolkata and then joined Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital, Pune in Jan 2016. Now she has completed 4 years in this Institute.

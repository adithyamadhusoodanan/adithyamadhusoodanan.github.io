    I am a Lecturer at the School of Mathematics within the University of Southampton where I am affiliated to CORMSIS and the OR Group. Prior to this, I was a Research Fellow at the University of Birmingham and had previously worked as a Research Associate at the Technical University of Freiberg. I have also had research stays at ORSTAT Centre - KU Leuven and at Wayne State University where I worked with Professor Boris S. Mordukhovich. After my BSc, MSc, and MPhil in Mathematics at the University of Yaounde I, I completed my PhD in Mathematics at the Technical University of Freiberg (with summa cum laude) under the supervision of Professor Stephan Dempe. I am a Fellow of the Alan Turing Institute, a Fellow of the IMA, a Fellow of the HEA, and a regular member of MOS, SIAM and The OR Society.
